Education

Education is wealth

One teacher,One book and One pen can change the world

Ironically, most industries in India are currently struggling with scarcity of skilled labour. The current education system does not focus on training young people in employable skills that can provide them with employment opportunities. With current and expected economic growth, this challenge is going to only increase further, since more than 75% of new job opportunities are expected to be “skill-based.” SRI VENKTESWARA EDUCATION AND SOCIAL WELFARE (SVESW) Trust aims to promote and provide vocational training to school leavers, existing workers, ITI graduates, Post Graduates etc. We endeavour to improve their employability by optimally utilizing the infrastructure available in Government, Private institutions and the Industry. It also builds capacity in the area of development of competency standards, course curricula, learning material and assessment standards in the country.
