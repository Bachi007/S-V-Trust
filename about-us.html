An incident of his school friend turned him in to a Social responsible person in the society. He believes that “EVERY SOLUTION COULD BE SOLVED FROM THE ORIGINATED ISSUE ITSELF”.


Many more friends of mine have tried discouraging me from shouldering such responsibilities and with good intention I am moving forward with my service. This is my ultimate social responsibilities everybody should feel this as a social obligation and heavy responsibility.


I always tried to provide my services to those who have a need from their basic needs. We endeavor to provide scholarships to the poor students and indicate skill development in the minds of the students by conducting workshops to the various fields. Through our organization we provide financial assistance to the needy students to establish themselves in various fields in their life. This organization assures you better life. Our Vision

Our vision towards the development of society especially rural communication by providing the facilities required to enhance their education and health for better livelihood.

What is SVESW Trust?

Proficiency in computers is an absolute requirement for success in today’s technology-driven environment. However, many youths simply do not know how to use a computer and are 100% computer illiterate. This project helps, by providing basic computer knowledge to youth and women in Chittoor and Nellore Districts. This essential training is in great demand, providing a much-needed foundation and equipping them for future success.SRI VENKATESWARA EDUCATION AND SOCIAL WELFARE TRUST has spread its hand to work for the betterment of youth and women in the best possible way. SVESW TRUST is an NGO, which is dedicatedly working to enhance the skill and welfare of every youth and women.

History

“We are Serving to the society from past 16 years as in many ways like, help homeless people,food supply for daily labour and needy people,donating blood etc.. as we are the trust members of Sri Venkateswara Education and Social Welfare Trust. It never fails in helping and giving a hand to needy people. In any crisis we love to help the poor. As of now we are coming officially as registered trust by the Govt Of Andhra Pradesh and we promise to the society we never ever give up in giving Hand to the needy.”

Rewards and Recognition


As I believe in helping poor and needy people, our local leaders irrespective of parties, encouraged and advised me to brighten up the organization. With the guidelines of the local leaders I registered the organization in the name of Sri Venkateswara Education and Social Welfare Trust in the year 2019. Many leaders appreciated all the hard work and diligent effort we have put in to work. It’s a great privilege to receive an honor from the great people and people who are working for the better society. I am greatful to have such people in our trust.
